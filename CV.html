Biography

I'm a PhD student in Machine Learning Group, Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto. My advisors are Prof. Raquel Urtasun and Prof. Richard Zemel. I work part-time as a Senior Research Scientist at Uber Advanced Technology Group Toronto. I am also affiliated with Vector Institute. I received M.Phil. degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Jiaya Jia. I got B.Eng. degree from School of Automation Science and Electrical Engineering in Beihang University (former Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics).

I have broad research interests in machine learning and computer vision. Recently I am focusing on the intersection of graphical models and neural networks, e.g., graph neural networks, generative models of graphs.

I've had the good fortune of working with some smart "neural networks".
