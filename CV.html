Biography

I am an Algorithm Engineer in the Department of Artifical Intelligence and Machine Learning at Shenzhen S.F. Technology, which is one of the bigest logistic companies in China. I am responsible for several object detection and OCR related projects, such as geometry information detection and scene text recognition, including traffic sign, lane, and panel detection.

I recieved my M.S. degree from Department of Biostatistics and Bioinformatics, Emory University in 2017. As the research assistant of Professor Hao Wu, I mainly focus on medical data mining, visualization and medical image recognition and diagnosis. I got my B.S degreen in Sun Yat-Sen University with double majors, Statistics and Mechanics.

I have board research interests in machine learning, statistical modeling and computer vision. My favorite topic is to further improve the capacity of machine learning algorithm to understand images, videos and texts in combination of statistical inference methods. Meanwhile, I also pay attention to the improvement of deep learning architectures and researches related to the application performance.

After two years in industry, I have rich experience in algorithm research, design, application and deployment. They motivate me to explore deeper, and also are the trigger for me to make a decision to return to campus. I am now seeking for a PhD position, please feel free to contact my if you have any interest, I am willing to talk at any time.
